Services & Specialisations

Specialist Service

Transmission Rebuilds & Reconditioning

Our core specialisation. We rebuild and recondition automatic and manual transmissions with precision — using quality parts and proven diagnostic methods. From slipping gears to complete failure, we identify the issue and repair it correctly.

  • Automatic transmission rebuilds
  • Manual gearbox repairs & overhauls
  • Transmission reconditioning
  • Fluid services & adjustments
  • Computerised fault diagnosis

General Mechanical Repairs

Comprehensive mechanical repairs carried out by experienced technicians. We diagnose accurately and return your vehicle to safe, reliable condition — with a full explanation of the work done.

Logbook Servicing

Manufacturer-compliant logbook services that preserve your new car warranty. Completed to specification and properly recorded — no dealer required.

Computer Diagnostics

Modern scan tools and diagnostic equipment for accurate fault detection. No guesswork — we identify the problem precisely before recommending any repair.

Brakes & Suspension

Complete brake system servicing including pads, rotors, calipers, and hydraulics. Suspension inspections, shock absorbers, struts, and steering components.

Engine & Drivetrain

From minor tune-ups to major engine repairs. Clutch replacement, driveshafts, differentials — all drivetrain components inspected and serviced correctly.

Classic Car Servicing & Restoration

Dave has genuine experience with classic and vintage vehicles — the quirks, the sourcing challenges, and the care these machines deserve. Your classic car is in safe hands.

4WD & SUV Servicing

Specialist knowledge of four-wheel drive systems, transfer cases, locking differentials, and the unique demands of 4WD and SUV drivetrains — on and off-road.

Pre-Purchase Inspections

Buying a used vehicle? A thorough mechanical assessment — including full transmission evaluation — gives you the complete picture before you commit. Essential for any second-hand purchase.

About the Workshop

A Workshop Built on Reputation

Located at 2/240 Parramatta Road, Burwood, we've established ourselves as one of Sydney's most trusted addresses for transmission work and general automotive repair. We're known for one thing above everything else: doing the job right.

Dave, the owner and lead mechanic, brings deep hands-on experience across a broad spectrum of vehicles — from modern computerised drivetrains to the carburettored engines of classic Australian and imported cars. His approach is consistent: diagnose correctly, explain everything clearly, and repair to a standard that lasts.

Transmission work is what sets us apart from most suburban workshops. It's a specialist area that many general mechanics prefer to avoid. We've invested in the expertise, tooling, and experience to handle it properly, and our growing reputation reflects that commitment.

We also have a genuine respect for classic vehicles. If you own a car that deserves patience and care rather than a rushed turnaround, this is the right workshop. Classic car owners return to us repeatedly because they know their vehicle is treated with the attention it deserves.

For everyday drivers, we offer the full range of mechanical services at fair, transparent pricing. No pressure. No unnecessary work. Just honest advice and reliable repairs from a local mechanic you can trust.
